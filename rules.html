COMMUNITY RULES

// OPERATIONAL DIRECTIVES //

RULE #01 No Exploits or Cheating

The use of third-party software, scripts, or macros to gain an unfair advantage in Edge Jump is strictly prohibited. Play fair or don't play at all.

RULE #02 Respect the Flow

Harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior towards other players or the SSOG development team will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

RULE #03 Build Integrity

Do not attempt to decompile, reverse engineer, or redistribute Pre-Alpha builds without official written consent from SSOG Studio.

RULE #04 Community Conduct

In our Discord and official channels, keep the discussion constructive. Spreading misinformation or false leaks about the project is not allowed.

VIOLATING THESE RULES WILL RESULT IN TERMINATION OF YOUR ACCESS TO THE GAME AND COMMUNITY.
