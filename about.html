Our Vision

SSOG is an independent game development studio born from the passion for movement and urban exploration. We don't just build games; we build adrenaline-fueled experiences that push the boundaries of virtual parkour.

Edge Jump Project

Our flagship title, Edge Jump, is a tribute to the "flow" state. We focus on realistic physics, high-stakes rooftops, and the feeling of absolute freedom. Every jump is calculated, every fall is a lesson.

The Studio

Operating from the shadows of digital space, SSOG thrives on community feedback. We believe in transparency and constant evolution, which is why we share our development journey every step of the way.
