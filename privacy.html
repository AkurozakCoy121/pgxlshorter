Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 03, 2026

1. Information We Collect

During the Pre-Alpha stage of Edge Jump, we may collect minimal data to improve the game experience:

  • System Specifications (OS, CPU, GPU, RAM) to optimize performance.
  • Crash Reports and Bug Logs.
  • Discord User IDs (if you join our community for tester roles).

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely for technical improvements. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers. All collected logs are used to identify and fix gameplay issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use external platforms for specific functions:

  • Saweria/Digital Wallets: We do not see your credit card or bank details. Transactions are handled securely by the respective payment gateways.
  • Discord: Subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please reach out to us via our official Discord channel.
